## Capital

| Date | Capital Ratio | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Jun 2022 | CET1 ~10.5% | Down from 10.9% prior year |
| Dec 2024 | Total Capital 15.3% | Adjusted Basel 3.1 estimate: 14.8% |
| — | RBNZ minimum CET1 | 4.5% (standardised banks) |

- Kiwibank uses the **standardised approach** for capital (not IRB like the Big Four)
- RBNZ 2025 capital review eased requirements for Group 2 banks (NZD $2–100B assets), worth ~NZD $500M to Kiwibank — directly enabling the scrapping of the equity raise
- Total regulatory capital: NZD $2.885B (Dec 2023) → NZD $3.363B (Dec 2024)
- Successful NZD $400M Tier 2 subordinated debt raise in 2025
