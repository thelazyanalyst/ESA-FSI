Divisional Performance

FY2024:

  • Institutional: Record revenue, record profit before provisions, record RoE ~14%
  • New Zealand: Cash NPAT A$2,286M for 12 months to Sep 2024 (up 1% on prior year); NIM declined 7bps
  • Australia Retail / Business & Private Bank: Underperformed; margin compression and competition

1H25:

  • Institutional: RoE 13%; Banking Business revenue +5%; Banking Business RoE 14%
  • New Zealand: Cash NPAT A$1,161M (6 months to Mar 2025), up 3% on prior half
  • Australia: First full half of Suncorp Bank earnings included

FY2025 (detailed divisional breakdowns in Investor Discussion Pack PDF):

  • Institutional and NZ consistently strong
  • Australia Retail and Business & Private Bank pressured by margin compression

Suncorp Bank Acquisition

  • Completed 31 July 2024
  • Adds significant Australian retail and business banking operations
  • FY25 net loans grew 13% YoY largely due to Suncorp consolidation
  • Customer migration to ANZ systems targeted for completion by June 2027
  • Integration charges contributed to significant items in FY25

Restructuring Charges (ANZ 2030 Strategy)

  • A$560M in restructuring charges taken in FY25
  • 3,500 roles cut (target: by Sep 2026); 60%+ completed by Q1 FY26
  • Exit 1,000 managed services consultants
  • Target: ~A$800M in gross pre-tax annual cost savings from FY26
  • Cost-to-income ratio below 50% at Q1 FY26

Regulatory Penalty Impact

  • A$250M combined penalties (Federal Court, December 2025) — ASIC's largest ever penalty against a bank
  • APRA Court Enforceable Undertaking: ~A$150M Root Cause Remediation Plan commitment
  • Combined ~A$400M one-off charge impact on FY25 / FY26 earnings
