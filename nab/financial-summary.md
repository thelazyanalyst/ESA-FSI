- NAB holds IRB (Internal Ratings-Based) accreditation from APRA
- Applies A-IRB, F-IRB, supervisory slotting, and standardised approaches across portfolios
- APRA AT1 phase-out from 1 Jan 2027: to be replaced by 0.25% more CET1 + 1.25% more Tier 2
